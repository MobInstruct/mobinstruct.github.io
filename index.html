Abstract

PartInstruct is a large-scale dataset designed to boost multi-modal models' ability to follow fine-grained, part-aware instructions. It contains over 1 million instruction-response pairs covering 80+ object categories and 700+ part types, and introduces a new benchmark for evaluating part-level instruction following. Extensive experiments show that models trained on PartInstruct achieve superior performance on part detection, grounding and reasoning tasks.
